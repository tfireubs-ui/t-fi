# Beat: Runes

## Scope

### Covers
- Runes protocol etching: new rune launches, minting activity
- Runes token transfers and market activity
- Runes marketplace metrics: floor prices, volumes, notable trades
- Infrastructure supporting runes: indexers, wallets, explorers, DEX integrations
- Runes protocol upgrades and governance changes
- Comparison of runes activity to BTC block space usage

### Does Not Cover
- BRC-20 activity (see ordinals beat — different token standard)
- General Bitcoin fee market (see bitcoin-macro beat)
- Ordinals inscription activity (see ordinals beat)

## Key Data Sources
- Runes explorers (ordiscan.com/runes, runescan.io)
- Unisat and Magic Eden (runes marketplace data)
- Bitcoin mempool.space (fee impact of runes activity)
- Runes indexer data (etching counts, minting progress)

## Vocabulary

### Use
- "etch," "etching," "mint," "burn," "transfer"
- "rune," "rune ID," "spacer," "divisibility"
- "minting progress," "supply cap," "premine"
- "UTXO," "output," "OP_RETURN"
- "cenotaph" (invalid rune transaction)

### Avoid
- Confusing runes with BRC-20 or ordinals (different standards)
- "ERC-20 on Bitcoin" framing — use "fungible token standard on Bitcoin"
- Speculative price targets
- "jpegs" (runes are not images)

## Framing Guidance
- Report runes activity with block-space context: how much of the Bitcoin block space did runes consume?
- Etching events are newsworthy when they launch tokens with significant premines or community traction.
- Minting completion is a material event for fungible token supply.
- Compare runes activity trends week-over-week to establish signal vs. noise.

## Example Signal

**Headline:** Runes minting activity accounts for 18% of Bitcoin block space in past week

**Signal:** Runes transactions consumed an average of 18% of Bitcoin block space over the seven days ending March 1, up from 9% the prior week. RSIC•GENESIS•RUNE led minting with 42,000 new mints, completing 67% of its supply cap. Average fee rates for runes transactions ran 2 sat/vB above the baseline block rate. Three new rune etchings launched with premines above 10% of total supply.
